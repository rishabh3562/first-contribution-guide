# 🛡️ Code of Conduct

We value open, inclusive, and respectful collaboration. By participating in this repository, you agree to follow these guidelines:

## Be Respectful
* Treat everyone with respect, regardless of experience, background, or opinions.
* Criticism should be constructive, focused on the work — not the person.

## Be Inclusive
* Welcome contributions from anyone — beginners and experts alike.
* Avoid discriminatory language or behavior.

## Be Responsible
* Report any issues or conflicts politely.
* Follow repository rules, including branch naming, PR guidelines, and issue creation.

## No Harassment
* Harassment, bullying, or abusive behavior will not be tolerated.
* This includes offensive comments, personal attacks, or inappropriate content.

## Enforcement
* Repository maintainers reserve the right to warn, block, or remove anyone violating this code.
* Contributors are encouraged to report violations to the maintainers.
